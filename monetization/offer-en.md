# AI PRD Risk Review

## I’ll review your AI-generated PRD for hallucinations, evidence gaps, and metric risks

AI can draft a PRD quickly. The expensive part is discovering later that it invented certainty, dropped a constraint, or used a metric nobody can actually calculate.

I will review one PRD, product strategy, or feature proposal and return a risk-first report that tells you:

- which claims lack evidence or a source;
- where assumptions are presented as facts;
- which metrics are missing a definition, denominator, owner, or time window;
- which constraints, launch criteria, or open questions may have disappeared;
- what requires human confirmation before the document is shared or implemented.

### Deliverables

- Review of up to 5,000 words or 15 pages
- Prioritized findings: critical, important, and low-risk
- Inline references to the affected sections
- A concise Markdown or PDF report
- One clarification/review round
- Delivery within 48 hours

### Short profile description

I help product managers and founders review AI-generated PRDs and strategy documents before unsupported claims, vague metrics, or missing constraints turn into expensive implementation mistakes. My reviews prioritize the few items that need human judgment instead of adding more AI-generated prose.
