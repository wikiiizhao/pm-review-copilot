# $100 Sprint - AI PRD Risk Review

Goal: close two AI-generated PRD or product-strategy review orders and net more than $100.

## Offer

**AI PRD Risk Review - Find Hallucinations, Missing Evidence, and Metric Gaps**

| Channel | Price | Estimated proceeds from two orders |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Upwork | $65 | At least about $110.50 at a 15% service fee, before payout costs |
| Fiverr | $65 | About $103 after 80% seller earnings and one $1 Bank Transfer |
| Direct PayPal | $75 | About $107.80 after two payment fees and one mainland-bank withdrawal |

These estimates exclude taxes, exchange-rate spreads, intermediary-bank fees, and fees that may differ by account. See [payment-and-pricing.md](payment-and-pricing.md).

## Customer receives

- Review of one document up to 5,000 words or 15 pages
- Unsupported claims, incomplete metrics, fact/assumption confusion, missing constraints, and revision drift
- Prioritized Markdown or PDF report
- One clarification round
- Delivery within 48 hours

## Fastest route

1. Publish [offer-en.md](offer-en.md) on Fiverr or Upwork using [platform-listings.md](platform-listings.md).
2. Contact founders and PMs who publicly discuss an AI product or PRD workflow using [outreach.md](outreach.md).
3. Attach [sample-audit.md](sample-audit.md).
4. Offer to review one sanitized paragraph for free, not the full document.
5. Confirm scope, receive payment on-platform, deliver, then request an honest review.

## What not to promise

- Independent verification of internal data you cannot access
- Legal, medical, financial, security, or regulatory compliance conclusions
- Full PRD rewriting inside the review package
- Public use of customer material without explicit permission

## Success metrics

Track only:

- targeted messages sent;
- meaningful replies;
- free paragraph checks completed;
- paid orders;
- net amount actually withdrawable.

Initial funnel target: 40 targeted contacts → 8 replies → 4 paragraph checks → 2 paid orders.
