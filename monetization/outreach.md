## Why not just ask AI to review it?

You absolutely can, and that is part of my workflow. The value is in applying a consistent review rubric, separating evidence from plausible-sounding claims, comparing revisions, and giving you a short list of human decisions instead of another long AI response. The sample lets you evaluate whether that difference is useful before paying.
