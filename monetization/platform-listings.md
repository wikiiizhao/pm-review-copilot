### Description

AI can draft a PRD quickly. The expensive part is discovering later that it invented certainty, dropped a constraint, or used a metric nobody can calculate.

I will review one PRD, product strategy, or feature proposal and return a concise, prioritized report covering:

- unsupported claims and missing evidence;
- assumptions presented as facts;
- metrics missing a definition, denominator, owner, source, or time window;
- missing constraints, launch gates, and rollback criteria;
- decisions that still require human confirmation;
- revision drift, when you provide an earlier version.

You receive a Markdown or PDF report with exact affected sections, recommended corrections, and a final decision checklist.

Scope: up to 5,000 words or 15 pages, one clarification round, delivered within 48 hours. Your material stays private and will not be used as a public sample without explicit permission.

This is a product-document review, not legal, medical, financial, security, or regulatory compliance advice.

### Buyer requirements

1. Upload the document as Word, PDF, Markdown, or a text-exported file.
2. What audience and decision should this document support?
3. Which supplied sources or data should the document rely on?
4. Do you want revision-drift checking? If yes, provide the previous version.
5. List any sections that are explicitly out of scope.
6. Confirm that you are authorized to share the supplied material.
